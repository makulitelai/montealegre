A gift. What truly is a gift? Is it just a thing where we get what we want on christmas or on our birthdays? Is it just our desire to have things? What truly is a gift? A gift is what a person gives to you on special occasions such as christmas and birthdays. A gift is not only a physical thing but can also be a talent, act, right or even the gift of life! A gift is what we appreciate when it is given to us because the person who gave it to us gave us his/her time and effort. No matter what gift it is, what truly matters is what the person's thoughts and compassion.
